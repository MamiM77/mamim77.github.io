About Me

I was born in Japan and migrated to Australia when I was 23 years old. I have Japanese parents and speak Japanese and English. I was an English teacher in Japan, then became a primary school teacher in Australia, teaching Japanese to primary school students. I live with a very kind- hearted Australian husband and two cats (Tilly and Yuki) in Victoria, Australia.

I love travelling, meeting people, and I love teaching Yoga and Meditation.

I have practised Yoga for over 10 years, then the COVID outbreak allowed me to take time to study Yoga in depth, leading me to become a qualified Yoga Instructor. I believe "connection" is a key to modern life. Connect to nature, connect to people and connect to yourself.
